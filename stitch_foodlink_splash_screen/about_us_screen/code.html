About Us

Our Mission

FoodLink's mission is to connect donors, NGOs, and receivers to reduce food waste and distribute surplus food effectively. We aim to create a sustainable food ecosystem where no edible food goes to waste.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where surplus food is efficiently distributed to those in need, minimizing environmental impact and fostering community support.

Our Team

FoodLink is powered by a dedicated team of individuals passionate about sustainability and social impact. We work tirelessly to ensure the app's functionality and effectiveness.
